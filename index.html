DreamShot Challenge

DreamShot is a startup challenge aiming to strengthen the youth budding entrepreneurs in Sri Lanka to transform their ideas into a real startup or scale the existing startup to the growth stage with extensive business coaching and field expertise with possible seed funding of up to 250,000 Sri Lankan rupees.
The Startup Challenge is co-organised by DreamSpace Academy in Batticaloa, Sri Lanka, and Sri Lanka Tamil Student Association, Chicago with the mission to empower the youth startup community in Sri Lanka who are less privileged and DreamShot co-creates with budding entrepreneurs’ or startup founders in making their dreams come true.
