1. Eligibility and requirements to participate

1.1 Eligibility

  1. You should be a SriLankan citizen
  2. Should be between the ages of 18 - 26 at the time of registration
  3. You can participate individually or as a team of a maximum of 3 team members.
  4. Any startup ideas or startups tackling the fundamental local challenges are welcomed to participate in the challenge
  5. If it is a startup, it should not be more than a year old to participate in the challenge.

1.2 Ineligible Individuals 

You are not eligible to participate in the DreamShot Startup  Challenge if you are:

  1. Under 18 years of age or over 26 years of age at the time of registration
  2. Not being a citizen in SriLanka

1.3 Proof of Eligibility

DreamSpace Academy reserves the right to verify your eligibility and ask for proof of eligibility at any time during the challenge.

1.4 Communications

All communication between DreamSpace Academy and you, including the DreamShot Startup Challenge website and email communication, must be in English. There are exceptions sometimes and at that time local languages will take part.

2. How to enter

2.1 Registration and participation

  1. To enter the Online Challenge, you must register at the  DreamShot Startup Challenge website and provide the required information about yourself, your startup idea or startup.
  2. The application will be a written application via Google form with 3 minutes pitch video (should not exceed 3 minutes) in any language but preferably in English.
  3. You may only register for the Online Challenge with one valid email address. If you compete in the same Online Challenge with multiple email addresses, you will be disqualified.
  4. You are not registered for the Online Challenge unless you receive a registration confirmation email from DreamSpace Academy.
  5. If you think you registered for the Online Challenge but did not receive a confirmation email, please email us with a subject line: DreamShot - followed by your query topic.
  6. If you miss any events please note the same event cannot be rescheduled or postponed for any reason.

2.2 Technical requirements

In order to enter the online challenge, you must have access to a stable internet connection, a valid email address, proper devices and ZOOM capability.

2.3 Future contact

In order to be contacted during the challenge or after, you must agree to provide your name, phone number, a valid postal address, and any other information DreamSpace Academy may need to invite you for an interview or programme.

3. DreamShot Startup Challenge structure

3.1 Overview

The programme encourages startup ideas and startups that work to solve fundamental local challenges in the community. It can be technological, or non-technological ideas or startups but it needs to have a social value proposition in the major thematic areas; Green Economy, Blue Economy, and Bio-Economy, but not limited to;

  • Alleviate poverty
  • Creates employability
  • Solve climate crisis
  • Enhance the traditional values
  • Reimagining education
  • Improved quality of life
  • Digital accessibility
  • Sustainable agriculture / Food production

3.2 Selection process

  1. Step 1 - 10 Teams will be selected from the received applications and will be called for a pitching session via Zoom with the panel of jurors, and 5 teams will be selected for the mentoring session.
  2. Step 2 - Shortlisted 5 Teams will be mentored to fine-tune their startup idea or to scale their startup with the mentors, and experienced related field expertise for 2 - 3 weeks.
  3. Step 3 - The 5 startup ideas/startups will be competing to become the top 3 startup ideas/startups to receive seed funding opportunities and further support.

3.3 Criteria

  1. Identify the problem or market opportunity
  2. Clearly highlight the innovative solution
  3. Describe the demographic market population for whom this is intended
  4. Demonstrate a feasible plan for going to market and being sustainable (e.g., as an initiative or business)

3.4 Mentoring and coaching

  1. The Selected 5 startup ideas/startups will be enrolled in 3 days deep-dive workshop with local and international mentors and business coaches from the organisers and partner organisations.
  2. Learning partnerships with mentors dedicated to the 5 teams on the relevant field/nature of the startup business and selected teams have to work with them.
  3. Business coaching with local and international experts will be provided and selected teams have to support them to tune your ideas.
  4. The 5 startup ideas/startups will be carried out with long term incubation/amplification support by DreamSpace Academy
  • Will be facilitated for local and international hackathons
  • Possible further funding opportunities
  • Support in further developing the growth business plan etc.

3.5 Modifying the DreamShot Startup Challenge

DreamSpace Academy or Srilankan Tamil Student Association may cancel or modify the structure, location, and times of the DreamShot Startup Challenge in its sole direction.

4. Awards and Recognition

4.1 Awards

  1. Monetary compensation will be provided to the three finalists as seed funding up to 250,000LKR.
  2. The Seed funding needs to be spent on the startup idea or startup.
  3. A milestone plan needs to be submitted for receiving the seed funding.
  4. Special recognition will be given to the other two teams for coming in the top 5 finalists.

4.2 Recognition

  1. The Startup that received the seed fundings and special recognition will be published in the Startup Sri Lanka portal by ICTA.
  2. The top 5 startups will be featured on the website of DreamSpace Academy.
  3. Media Coverage to feature the startups in the news channels.
  4. The top three winners will be announced at the annual Iyal Isai Natyam produced by STSA in Chicago, USA.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do we need to pay any money to register for the startup challenge?

    No, Entry to the DreamShot Startup Challenge is free of charge.

  • Do we need to have any specific qualifications to be a participant in the startup challenge?

    No, there is no qualification required. We are looking for highly motivated budding entrepreneurs and fresh startups.

  • What are the prizes of the DreamShot Startup Challenge?

    The top 10 finalists will be provided with Certificates, and Top 3 finalists will be provided with seed funding up to Sri Lanka Rupees 250,000/-. The monetary award will be decided by the panel of jurors.

  • How will the prize money be awarded?

    The prize money will be given into tranches according to the milestone plan agreed by the finalist and the organizers according to the development of the startup.

  • If it is a team submission, does that mean all team members will have to be a part of the pitching video?

    No, a Team Leader can do the pitch video for submission. Anyhow, we love to see all the team members faces on the pitch video :)

  • Do you allow one person to participate in two teams?

    No, one participant can participate in one team.

  • How many submissions can be made by an individual or a team?

    Only one submission can be submitted by an individual or a team.
