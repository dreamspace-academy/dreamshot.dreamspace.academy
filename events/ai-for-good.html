Unlock the Power of AI

The AI for Good Exhibition is a transformative 4-day event focused on showcasing the immense potential of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in solving societal challenges. This exhibition brings together researchers, students, and innovators from universities, initiatives, and organizations to share their work, inspire new ideas, and promote AI’s positive impact on the world. Through engaging interactions with diverse audiences, including school students and young innovators, the event fosters an inclusive platform for learning, collaboration, and growth.

  • Event Objectives

  • Universities and Initiatives’ Stalls:

  • Training Programs for School Students
  • DreamSpace Projects:

We aim to promote AI research by showcasing cutting-edge innovations from universities and initiatives, inspire young minds with hands-on training and project platforms, and foster collaborations among students, academics, and professionals.


  • Promote AI Research and Innovation: Offering a space for universities and initiatives to display their cutting-edge AI research and products.
  • Encourage Skill Development: Hands-on AI training sessions for school students, building foundational knowledge and practical skills. .
  • Inspire Young Innovators: A platform for children to present AI-powered projects, sparking creativity and confidence in young minds.
  • Build Networks: Facilitate networking opportunities among academics, industry professionals, and students, encouraging future collaborations. .

Universities and initiatives will present their AI research, products, and innovations through engaging interactive stalls, allowing attendees to explore advancements, connect with researchers, and discover practical AI applications.


  • Universities and initiatives will showcase their AI research, products, and innovations through interactive stalls.
  • Attendees can explore these innovations, engage with researchers, and learn about practical AI applications.

Three hands-on training sessions will be conducted daily, each limited to 25 participants. These workshops will cover AI fundamentals, ethical considerations, and real-world applications, equipping students with essential AI knowledge.


  • Three hands-on training sessions per day, limited to 25 participants per session.
  • Workshops will cover AI fundamentals, ethical AI, and real-world applications, empowering students with valuable AI knowledge. .

DreamSpace projects will be showcased, with volunteers from The Path organization guiding attendees through the initiatives. Trainees will also support the stalls, sharing insights and assisting visitors.


  • A display of DreamSpace projects, where volunteers from The Path organization will guide attendees through the initiatives.
  • Trainees will also provide support at the stalls, sharing knowledge and assisting visitors.
