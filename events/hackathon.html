Embark on a transformative 3-day AI Hackathon, where innovation meets collaboration. Event Highlights

Unleash your creativity and build cutting-edge AI systems!

Preliminary Round (Online)

  • Submit your ideas online to get shortlisted for the hackathon.

Day 1: Kickoff

  • Introduction to AI and short talks by industry experts.
  • Hackathon begins and continues until the end of the day.

Day 2: Development

  • Teams discuss challenges faced on Day 1.
  • Continue the hackathon until the end of the day.

Day 3: Finale

  • Final deployment and submissions by afternoon.
  • Short talks and experience sharing during the evaluation.
  • Awarding ceremony to recognize the best teams.
