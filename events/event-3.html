Film Screenings & Discussions

Immerse yourself in thought-provoking cinema and engage in in-depth discussions with renowned filmmakers at the Film Screenings & Discussions segment of the Creativepreneurs Festival. This is your chance to explore unique narratives, learn from industry experts, and gain insights into the creative process behind impactful films.

  • Munnel by Visakesa Chandrasekaram
  • Tentigo by Ilango Ram & Hiranya Ram
  • Demon in Paradise by Jude Ratnam

Munnel by Visakesa Chandrasekaram

Q&A on storytelling and creative challenges

Join Visakesa Chandrasekaram for an engaging Q&A session where he delves into the complexities of storytelling in filmmaking. Gain a deeper understanding of the creative challenges filmmakers face when bringing a story to life on screen.

Tentigo by Ilango Ram & Hiranya Ram

Insights into creative processes and inspiration

Discover the creative journey behind Tentigo as Ilango Ram and Hiranya Ram share the inspirations, challenges, and processes involved in creating their cinematic masterpiece. Learn how they translate ideas into visual storytelling.

Demon in Paradise by Jude Ratnam

A documentary exploring Sri Lankan history and reconciliation

Experience Demon in Paradise, a powerful documentary by Jude Ratnam that takes an intimate look at Sri Lanka's tumultuous past and the ongoing journey toward reconciliation. Gain new perspectives on Sri Lankan history and the art of documentary filmmaking.

Music & Sound Workshops

Dive into the transformative world of music and sound with a series of immersive workshops designed to inspire and educate. Whether you're a seasoned musician or a curious beginner, these sessions offer something for everyone

  • Songwriting & Hip-Hop Workshop
  • Music Production & Cultural Discussion
  • Sound Therapy Session

Songwriting & Hip-Hop Workshop

Navz-47 & Kaber Vasuki

Discover the art of crafting powerful lyrics and melodies in this interactive workshop. With a special focus on Hip-Hop, Navz-47 and Kaber Vasuki will share their unique approaches to songwriting, helping you turn emotions and stories into compelling music.

Music Production & Cultural Discussion

Imaad Majeed

Learn cutting-edge music production techniques while exploring how cultural traditions influence modern sounds. Imaad Majeed will guide participants through blending technology and heritage to create innovative and meaningful music.

Sound Therapy Session

Rachel Chanmugam

Experience the healing potential of sound in this therapeutic workshop. Rachel Chanmugam will introduce you to the science and practice of sound therapy, demonstrating how frequencies and vibrations can be used to relax, rejuvenate, and balance the mind and body.

Creative Storytelling Workshops

Step into the world of storytelling with workshops designed to unleash your creative potential. Whether you're a budding storyteller, a seasoned writer, or a filmmaker looking to refine your narrative skills, these sessions will equip you with the tools to craft compelling and impactful stories.

  • One-Liner Pitch Workshop
  • Building Stories Workshop

One-Liner Pitch Workshop

Master the art of crafting impactful one-sentence pitches that capture attention and leave a lasting impression. Perfect for filmmakers, writers, and creatives, this workshop sharpens storytelling skills and helps present ideas effectively to producers, publishers, or audiences.

Building Stories Workshop

Transform your one-liner pitch into a detailed story outline by structuring ideas, developing characters, and crafting a captivating narrative arc. With expert guidance, refine your concept into a production-ready masterpiece.
