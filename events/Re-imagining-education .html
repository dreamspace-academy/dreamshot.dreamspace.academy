Youth-Led Innovations

Youth-led innovations focus on reimagining education through affordable DIY teaching aids, inclusive technology, and assistive tools for better learning.

Cultural Fusion Hackathon

The Cultural Fusion Hackathon blends tradition and innovation, encouraging participants to design educational tools like gamified STEM games.

Design educational tools blending tradition and innovation, like gamified board games for STEM learning. Featuring Dr. Gihan Dias and Sanjeev Jha.

Educator Training

Educator Training focuses on equipping teachers with skills to integrate innovative teaching methods while respecting local traditions and cultures.

Equip teachers to integrate maker education while honoring local traditions with Dr. Harsha Alles (Gateway Group) and Shyamala Gomez (inclusive education advocate)
