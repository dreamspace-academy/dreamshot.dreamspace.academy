Reimagining Education

Re-imagining Education combines traditional knowledge and modern technology, fostering creativity and innovation through hands-on learning, problem-solving, and cultural integration to create a more inclusive and impactful future of education.

Workshops and Demonstrations

STEAM workshops and hands-on learning for students and teachers. Highlight alternative education models and MakerSpace tools.

Storytelling Through Making

Storytelling Through Making combines creativity and craftsmanship, using tools like AR and folklore to craft immersive, meaningful narratives.

Learning Zones for Local Solutions

Learning Zones for Local Solutions focus on tackling community challenges like water management and waste reduction through expert collaboration.

Youth-Led Innovations

Youth-led innovations focus on reimagining education through affordable DIY teaching aids, inclusive technology, and assistive tools for better learning..

Cultural Fusion Hackathon

The Cultural Fusion Hackathon blends tradition and innovation, encouraging participants to design educational tools like gamified STEM games.

Educator Training

Educator Training focuses on equipping teachers with skills to integrate innovative teaching methods while respecting local traditions and cultures.
