Re-imagining Education

Re-imagining Education combines traditional knowledge and modern technology, fostering creativity and innovation through hands-on learning, problem-solving, and cultural integration to create a more inclusive and impactful future of education.

Workshops and Demonstrations

STEAM workshops and hands-on learning for students and teachers. Highlight alternative education models and MakerSpace tools.

Traditional Crafts Meet Tech: Learn weaving, pottery, and batik-making with modern tools like 3D printing and laser cutting. Featuring Deshan Kuruvita-Aratchy (Fab Lab Colombo) and Anoma Wijewardene (renowned textile artist).
Agriculture & Sustainability: Explore smart farming with IoT and traditional techniques, led by Dr. Ajith De Alwis (COSTI) and Dilhan C. Fernando (CEO, Dilmah Tea).
